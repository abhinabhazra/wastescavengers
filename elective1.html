REFLECTIONS ELECTIVE 1

 

Are there any materials we use more now than we did before?

 
Paper bags are gaining popularity over plastic due to environmental reasons. People prefer them because they are biodegradable and less harmful, aligning with the growing focus on sustainability.

 

Give an example of a positive change and a negative one?

 
A positive change in the environment could be the increased adoption of renewable energy sources, leading to a reduction in greenhouse gas emissions and mitigating climate change. On the negative side, deforestation, especially in crucial ecosystems like the Amazon rainforest, contributes to habitat loss, biodiversity decline, and carbon dioxide release, negatively impacting the environment.

 

What gets people to make that positive change?

 
Financial incentives, awareness campaigns, supportive policies, technological advancements, community engagement, corporate initiatives, and infrastructure development collectively drive the increased adoption of renewable resources.
