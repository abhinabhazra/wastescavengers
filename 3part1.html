Part 1

Where Does My Waste Go

 

Procedure

To investigate what happens to waste in our school, after it's been put in a bin, by talking to our teachers, the adminsitration, staff and cleaners.

 

Where Does My Waste Go?

In this activity, we conducted interviews with various individuals, including cleaners, teachers, and administrative staff, to learn about the disposal of collected waste. Through our interviews and a series of questions, we discovered that the school has a two-part waste management process. First, the waste collected in bins is used for composting within the school. Second, the remaining waste is picked up by the municipal corporation. Interestingly, we also found that the school's waste disposal methods differ from those used by individuals at home.
